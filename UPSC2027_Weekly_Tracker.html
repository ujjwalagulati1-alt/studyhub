The 5 Non-Negotiable Rules
  • 1
    Coverage before depth — touch all 16 Prelims clusters before going deep into any.
  • 2
    No method without a cluster — every session tagged to backbone. Floating study doesn't count.
  • 3
    Sunday red flag — any cluster untouched 2+ weeks = Monday's first session, no exceptions.
  • 4
    Mains minimum — at least 1 written answer per week, every week, from Month 1.
  • 5
    Bad score protocol — after a bad mock, 48-hr reflection only. Then reset. No spiralling.
