The 7 Non-Negotiable Rules

📌 The core 7

  • PYQ first, always — Questions drive what you read and how deep
  • Study Hub is sacred — Every session ends with at least 1 entry. Back up every Sunday.
  • Revise more than you learn — 40% learning, 60% revision. Study Hub tracker enforces this.
  • Attempt every Vision test — Never postpone because "not ready." Attempt cold. Review is the learning.
  • Wednesday = Optional — Non-negotiable. Economics Optional needs consistent dedicated time.
  • 10 PM is the wall — Sleep is memory consolidation. A tired brain studying is worse than not studying.
  • Sunday report to A — 3 numbers: Study Hub entries, Mains answers written, Sandhan accuracy %.

🛡 The 5 bad-day protocols

  • Bad test score: 48-hr reflection maximum. Write one thing you learned. Then reset. No spiralling.
  • Missed 2 days: Don't try to "catch up" in double sessions. Resume normal schedule. Two-day rule: never miss a third.
  • Syllabus anxiety: Open Study Hub. Count your entries. You're building something real. Then do just the next PYQ.
  • Travel / illness: CA + Hindu reading only. No structured study. Built into the plan — not a disruption.
  • Confidence below 5/10: Name one win from this week first (to A, on Sunday). Then plan. Never plan from anxiety.
